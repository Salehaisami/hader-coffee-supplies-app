Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2025

Hader ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Hader mobile application. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

When you create an account or place an order, we collect:

  • Phone number — used for authentication via OTP verification
  • Business name and contact information — used to identify your account and communicate about orders
  • Delivery location (coordinates and address details) — used to fulfill your orders
  • Order history — used to display your past orders and improve our service

How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity and secure your account
  • To process and deliver your orders
  • To communicate with you about order status and updates
  • To improve our app and services

Location Data

We request your device location only during checkout to help you set a delivery pin. Your coordinates are stored to fulfill delivery and reused on future orders for convenience. You can update your saved location at any time. We do not track your location in the background.

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share your delivery details only with our fulfillment partners (delivery drivers and suppliers) to the extent necessary to complete your order.

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase infrastructure. We retain your account and order data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services.

Payments

Payments via Apple Pay are processed securely by Apple. We do not store your payment card details. Cash on Delivery orders do not involve any digital payment data.

Your Rights

You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us. We will process your request within 30 days.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
